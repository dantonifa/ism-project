About Us

The High School Salvador Moncada has its name as a tribute to the Honduran Doctor, Sir Salvador Moncada, who did many contributions to the Science in the fields of inflammation, vascular biology, and cell growth. Precisely, the mission of Salvador Moncada High School is to prepare the students for advancing in their academic careers to contribute to the society in the fields where they are talented for, wether be Science or Arts.

Address

The High School Salvador Moncada is located in the city of San Pedro Sula, Honduras; in the neighborhood of Barrio Rio de Piedras, southwest of the city, 9th Street, 23rd and 24th avenues, in front of Dryclean USA. Barrio Rio de Piedras is beside the mountain known as El Merendon, habitat of many amazing animals like the emerald hummingbird, the rainbow toucan, and the gem beetle, all of them have visited the high school.
